Patatas bravas

Description

Nothing screams tapas more than nice, freshly fried potato cubes with salsa brava

Ingredients

  • 2kg Potatoes
  • 1 large onion
  • 4-5 garlic cloves
  • 1 Tbpn sweet paprika
  • 1 Tbpn smoked/spicy paprika (or ground cayenne)
  • 1 Tbsn flour
  • 250 ml broth (chicken, veggie...)
  • Oil for frying
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Peel and chop into 2-3cm cubes all the potatoes, wash and dry
  2. In a large pot, heat enough oil for frying, and once it's hot add the potatoes. Fry until golden, remove and let soak in paper. Add salt while hot
  3. For the sauce, peel and chop in large chunks the garlic and onion
  4. In a pan with about 4 tablespoons of hot oil, fry the garlic, then the onion, careful to not burn them
  5. When the onion and garlic are golden, add the flour and mix until the flour is cooked
  6. Add both paprika and the broth, mix well and cook for a couple minutes
  7. Blend all the ingredients together until smooth
  8. Add the sauce to the potatoes and enjoy!
